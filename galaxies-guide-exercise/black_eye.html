Galaxy Guide

Black eye galaxy

Description

The Black Eye galaxy (Messier 64, NGC 4826) is a spiral galaxy located approximately seventeen million light-years (about 5.2 megaparsecs) away in the constellation Berenice's Hair.

Interesting data

  1. Scientists believe in the hypothesis that it is the result of the collision between two galaxies.
  2. The spiral galaxy was discovered by Edward Pigott on March 23, 1779
  3. Can be seen with good binoculars
