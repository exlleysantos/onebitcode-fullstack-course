Magellanic Cloud

Description

The Large Magellanic Cloud (commonly abbreviated as LMC, from the English Large Magellanic Cloud) is a satellite dwarf galaxy that orbits around the Milky Way.

Interesting data

  1. It is rich in gases and dust
  2. It was baptized by Fernão de Magalhães
  3. The Large Magellanic Cloud is one of the closest galaxies to the Milky Way
